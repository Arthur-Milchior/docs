Developer Guides
====================================
Jina developer guides cover specific features and use cases.

Jina Hub is a centralized registry, where you can share and discover custom and community driven Jina Pods and Apps tailored to specific use cases of neural search.

Brand new users to Jina Hub should first begin with the :doc:`../introduction/index` section which covers more fundamental concepts.

Further information on any specific method can be obtained in the :doc:`../api_references/index`.
